EECS Grades at University of Michigan (Incomplete)

The Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) department at the University of Michigan is one of it's biggest. According to the department's website,

The Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) Department is among the leading departments of its kind in the nation, with a diverse program of over 100 courses per semester, 143 faculty members, 1880+ undergraduate, and almost 1100 graduate students

Because of this, I thought it'd be cool to see how students fare grade and course wise in this department

Grade Data

The grade data used in this blog post was obtained directly from the University of Michigan itself and covers the following four semesters: Winter and Fall for the years 2016 and 2017.

It is also the same data we use for course distribution visualisations over at the Michigan Daily GradeGuide.
